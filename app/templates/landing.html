Everything in one place

Purpose-built for how CA firms actually staff work

No spreadsheets, no guessing who's free. splanly handles the complexity of resourcing so you can focus on the client.

Team management

Maintain a complete roster of partners, directors, managers, staff, and articles — with roles, seniority, and availability tracked in one place.

Engagement tracking

Manage audits, tax filings, consulting projects, and special assignments with recurrence patterns and instance-based scheduling.

Smart allocation

Assign staff to engagements with automatic over-allocation checks. The system flags conflicts before they happen, not after.

Leave management

Track vacation, sick leave, and exam leave. Approved leave automatically blocks any assignment that would conflict with it.

Bench dashboard

See instantly who's on the bench right now, and who's rolling off soon — with configurable windows for proactive planning.

Role-based access

Admin, moderator, and viewer roles with Microsoft 365 SSO and two-factor authentication built in for enterprise security.
