Everything You Need

Purpose-built for CA firms, StaffPlan handles the complexities of resource management so you can focus on what matters.

Team Management

Maintain a complete roster of partners, directors, managers, staff, and articles with roles, seniority, and availability tracking.

Engagement Tracking

Manage audits, tax filings, consulting projects, and special assignments with recurrence patterns and instance-based scheduling.

Smart Allocation

Assign staff to engagements with automatic over-allocation prevention. The system blocks conflicts before they happen.

Leave Management

Track vacation, sick leave, and exam leave. Approved leaves automatically block conflicting assignments.

Bench Dashboard

Instantly see who's on the bench now and who's rolling off soon. Configurable windows for proactive planning.

Role-Based Access

Admin, moderator, and viewer roles with Microsoft 365 SSO and two-factor authentication for enterprise security.
